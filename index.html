About

Greetings, I'm Austin Lang. As of 2021, I'm a sophomore at Brown University, concentrating in Computer Science for an Sc.B. degree and Economics for an A.B. degree. I feel that these two fields complement each other perfectly and can apply to a myriad of real-world problems. Furthermore, the crossroad between these two subjects encompasses my interests, abilities, and experiences.

I have a passion for solving problems, especially in a creative fashion. However, no problem is ever best solved alone. Throughout my life, I have strived to be the best leader and collaborator that I can possibly be: my development has stemmed from the various projects, extracurricular activities, and work opportunities that I am proud and thankful to have partaken in.

Learning, self-improvement, and persistence are the driving forces behind my daily life.

Coronavirus Fundraiser Project

This project was founded and led by me during the peak of the COVID-19 Pandemic in March 2020, when medical supplies were sparse in Boston. Our team comprised of the leaders of a few large student organizations and faculty members. We hosted a variety of fundraising events and secured a supply chain with contacts in China so that supplies like masks and gloves could be delivered. These supplies were then donated to the surrounding Boston area.
